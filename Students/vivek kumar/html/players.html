About BCCI

The Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) was set up on December 4, 1928 by a group of players at Delhi's Roshnara Club to end the British monopoly in cricket. BCCI had 6 regional bodies as its first members. Today it has 30 full-time members, and is worth Rs 3,308 crore. For more information click here
